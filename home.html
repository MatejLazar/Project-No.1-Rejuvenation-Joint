What is Destructuring?

Destructuring is a form of stretching and it is really all about releasing the tension by reaching the apex of the strech. Once that limit has been reached one should experience tremour. A tremour happens when muscle begins to clutch and release rapidly without intentional doing. The result is deep muscle relaxation, therefore we highly recommend you to practice Destructuring prior to any other technique in order to achieve the better results!

In the Destructuring Dojo, you will be thorougly guided through 15 different postures by which you will stretch all the major muscle groups of your body, get yourself energized and ready to face your daily challenges.

What is Guided Meditation?

Simply put, a guided meditation is a meditation led by meditation facilitator or a teacher. It can be either guided in person or via audio or video. There are so many different forms of meditation, and none of them is right or wrong. It is very individual journey, which like any other, requires your time and effort.

Here in Rejuvenation Joint, we have decided to start with simple techniques which will serve as a building ground for more advanced material.

There are two major types of meditation. Active and passive. The active meditation is more of an advanced form of meditation, it requires more focus and mastery of all the basics in order for proper execution, while the passive meditaion is more subtle, suitable for the beginners, therefore we are going to introduce you to Passive Seated Meditation in the upcoming videos.

What is The Alexander Technique?

The Alexander Technique teaches us to observe ourselves and prevent the habitual use of oneself. It is very similar to the Guided meditation as it has some of the same, and some similar elements within the practice. Unlike Destructuring or Guided meditation, we would advise you to find a licenced techique teacher because nothing is as good as the directions teacher's hands can give you at the beginning. Few weeks with a teacher is going to save you atleast year of determined practive and research.

The Alexander Technique is a practice that has to be done consitantly, on the daily basis in order to bring forth the positive changes into your life because unfortunately, it is not so easy to unlearn old habits and behaviors. Our goal here is to expand awareness of the technique and possibly help you experiece some of those, positive impacts you can have by regular practice.
